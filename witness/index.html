A new intranet

The digital workplace project aims to give everyone at Citizens Advice easy access to the information, knowledge, and tools they need to do their jobs. As part of this, we're building a new Intranet to replace CABlink and BMIS. When it's live, you'll be able to access the new intranet from this page.

In the meantime, you can explore what will live on the new intranet and what should be on another platform or channel.
